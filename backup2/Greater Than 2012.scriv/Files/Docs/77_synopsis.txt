By this point, internet has become spotty, but TV and radio continues to report garbled updates.  Rebekkah manages to tune into an AM channel broadcasting news that several groups have claimed to have found patterns in the numbers.  The crowd outside listens from a speaker pointed out the back door.  A fear grips SUMC, fearing that "they're on to us", and McGregor insists that they must hide Paul.  The group argues over the severity of this news.  While McGregor becomes hardened, even angry, the crowd waits for Paul to deliver a 'message', but naturally he resists.
