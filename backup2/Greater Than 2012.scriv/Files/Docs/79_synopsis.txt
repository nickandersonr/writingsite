The Quartet nervously follows the broadcast from inside the living room, a careful eye on the crowd in the backyard.  The group is discussing options when McGregor enters to inform them that they are 'moving out'.  The view of the crowd outside is horrifying - armed with makeshift weapons from the Hollis' tool shed and wielding giant banners declaring "Paul Hollis is Lord", they intend to forcefully set the record straight against the 330 other 'cults' ignoring the word of Paul, Lebe, and therefore God.  Paul decries their actions but has been usurped by McGregor, declaring himself the first prophet of the Later New Testemant, who has pivoted his number theory to make himself their new leader.  They march off the premises singing a battle hymn.
